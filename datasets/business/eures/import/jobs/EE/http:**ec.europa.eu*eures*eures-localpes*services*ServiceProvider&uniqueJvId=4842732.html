Title: SWEDISH SPEAKING HVAC ENGINEER
Description:
Amhold Ltd. has been active in the construction industry since 1992. During these years, we have become one of the biggest companies on the Estonian construction market providing architectural and engineering services and taking active part in the realisation of international projects. Amhold Ltd. mainly designs construction plans and deals with project management, construction supervision, construction site consulting, expertise and surveys. At Amhold we work with industry leading architects and design firms to provide high quality engineering services for public and private clients and are seeking a motivated individual to join the team. The candidate should be experienced in engineering projects from concept to completion, collaborating with architects and designers to achieve engineering/architectural solutions, developing system design options, and attending and leading project design and site construction meetings. Key Responsibilities: Capable of completing full HVAC and plumbing design tasks in accordance with Swedish legislation and regulations Required skills and qualification: Bachelor Degree from an accredited university in a mechanical discipline Proficiency in Swedish and English Professional experience as a senior designer at least 3 years Proficiency in AutoCad, MagiCad and/or other similar programs Possess a strong technical knowledge of HVAC air and water side systems which should include duct systems, ventilation, piping systems, and automated controls. Perform calculations, conduct code research, and create schematic and final designs Ability to interface with architects and designers We Offer: Professional, highly motivated and friendly team-members International work experience Additional requirements: Self- reliance in work Excellent knowledge of Swedish construction field regulation, legislation and project design practice Additional information: Start date: ASAP Contract length: Permanent/Project-based Contact: Contact name: HR specialist Maris Palu Telephone:+372 6996 995 E-mail: maris.palu@amhold.eu Homepage: http://www.amhold.ee/?lng=en
Required languages: (ENGLISH-very good, SWEDISH-very good)
