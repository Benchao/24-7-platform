Title: NORWEGIAN CUSTOMER SERVICE REPRESENTATIVES
Description:
Runway International Runway International Runway is a modern International Customer Contact Center located in the city center of Tallinn. We provide a variety of communication related services to medium and large sized corporations around the world. Runway is looking for Norwegian speakers to work for us on the HURTIGRUTEN project in Tallinn. Hurtigruten is the pride of Norway and the most beautiful sea journey in the world! Our other major customers include some of the world known brands like Norwegian Air. NORWEGIAN CUSTOMER SERVICE REPRESENTATIVES We offer: Fun and exciting English / Nordic speaking working environment Salary above Estonian average / Opportunity to earn additional Bonus Possibility to work full/part-time Flexible working hours/Possibility to work on the weekends and in the evenings Possible training trips abroad Assistance with finding accommodation in Tallinn if needed Career opportunity within the organization Employee discounts (sport clubs; restaurants; company events; special phone operator tariff; sport clubs; restaurants; etc ) Bright and modern working environment If you are: Fluent in Norwegian Have a passion for Sales/Customer Service/Motivated by helping people Interested in Tourism / Cruises / Hospitality Want to develop your communication skills with our professional trainers … we will be glad to welcome you in our team Responsibilities: •Receive phone calls/ e-mails on behalf of Runway’s clients and partners •Perform service requirements of Runways customer providing an efficient, polite, friendly and honest response to their queries and requests for information •Provide relevant information in a professional and timely manner in order to maximize sales and achieve performance objectives •Work in a team of Customer Service Agents or Customer Sales Specialist to achieve and over achieve the team’s performance targets •Attend specified training, organized by Runway or the customer, in order to develop professional work and individual standards, to improve skills of business relationship with clients and customers, develop the skills and experiences necessary to improve product knowledge and increase sales and service ability Education required Basic education Languages required English, Norwegian Work experience required 0-2 years Working time Full-time work, Part-time work Start of work Negotiable Location Tallinn City Center Category Tourism / Hotels / Catering Position Specialist Contact person Heilika Kütt, cv@runway.ee Address Tartu mnt 63, Tallinn Telephone +372 6681062 Fax +372 6681068 URL http://www.runway.ee
