Title: TECHNICAL PROJECT MANAGEMENT
Description:
The Centre for Ecological Research and Forestry Applications (CREAF) is a public research institution that was created in 1987 by the Generalitat (Autonomous Government) of Catalonia, the Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB) and the Institute of Catalan Studies (IEC), to promote basic and applied research in terrestrial ecology. The University of Barcelona (UB) subsequently joined in. CREAF seeks a professional in the field of management research projects, especially Europeans. Main functions of the job: - Promote the participation of researchers in new research projects (search for new funding opportunities) - Participation in the request of proposals for new projects - Control and monitoring of the different projects of the center - Administrative support and management, participating in the supervision of different projects - Participation in the preparation of justification reports - Participation and support in the preparation of audits. PROFIL OF EMPLOYEE:Degree in Business Administration, Economics or similar. Or alternatively, a Degree in Biology, Environmental Science, Forest Engineering or similar, with possession of Master`s Degree in Administrative Management. Minimum one year experience in tasks related to project management, having knowledge of the different research programs: FP7, INTERREG, EUROSTARS, LIFE,  Required proof of English level (certificate) High level of Spanish and Catalan. We assessed the knowledge of other EU languages.
