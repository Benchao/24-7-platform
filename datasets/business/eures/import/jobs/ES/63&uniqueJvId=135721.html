Title: ACTIRIS : Entertainers with German language skill (with or without experience) to work in Spain/Canarias (Fuerteventura). / Entertainers with German
Description:

FRANCAIS

We are a service company that has been devoted to the leisure sector since 1990. Its main areas of activity are tourist entertainment and party and event organisation. We are present in Spain's main tourist regions and we have offices in the Canary Islands, Balearic Islands and Andalusia with excellent human resources and logistical infrastructures.

Our team includes highly qualified professionals with long experience in the sector. This professionalism allows us to research, innovate and update our leisure and entertainment projects constantly. Join our team and you'll form part of a fun, buoyant company.

We select personnel to work as tourist entertainers in hotels on the Spanish coast (Balearics, Canaries and Andalusia) after four weeks of training paid for by the company.

The requirements are as follows - languages and artistic or sporting skills:
- As part of this service we select, train and contract personnel, draw up programmes and supervise the service. Tourist entertainers are responsible for carrying out the entertainment programme at hotels: sports activities, leisure activities and shows.
- If you have artistic abilities (singing, dancing, crafts, juggling, acting, etc.) you could develop these and turn your favourite sport or hobby into an important element of your new profession.
- This time you don't need any experience, as we will provide selected candidates with an intensive 4 week training course free of charge to teach them the techniques and skills of this magnificent profession.
- We are looking for creative, dynamic, flexible individuals who are good at communicating, enthusiastic and keen to enjoy their job.
- We are looking urgent for entertainers with German language skill (with or without experience) for work in our hotels in Canarias. (Fuerteventura).

Offers:
- We offer the meals and accommodations.
- The salary depends on your experience.
