Title: ACTIRIS : Customer Support Advisers, (m/v).
Description:


NEDERLANDS

We are currently recruiting Customer Support Advisers, (m/v) to be part of an exciting new team, working for the leader in the technological sector (computers, MP3, telecommunication, entertainment).
We are a dynamic company in constant growth.

Profil of employee:
Our Service will be responsible for resolving Customer's technical requests, when possible, in a single telephone call using customer's troubleshooting protocols.
 
Main tasks :
- Take incoming user requests (via telephone, e-mail, voicemail, pager, or other automated alerts), log call details onto case management systems and provide response and resolution within SLA to customer
- Maintain knowledge and expertise associated with applications specific to Users.
- Progress / close service requests to a satisfactory conclusion on call management system.
- Proactively update customers with status and resolution progress.
- Monitor / progress all open queries in queue.
- Escalate potential service issues to appropriate level.
- Respond to any reasonable task posed by the assigned manager.
- Achieve an excellent customer satisfaction level.
 
Requirements:
- English compulsory at near native level, spoken and written skill.
- Dutch at native level.
- Excellent customer care skills.
- Advanced user knowledge of IT environment.
- Windows and Mac environment literate.
- Adequate professional experience.
- 1 year in a customer service environment, preferably technical support
- IT knowledge. 
-  Excellent customer care knowledge and awareness.
- Ability to implement and manage changes in processes.
- Service passion.
- Active listening and identification of sales potential .
- Responsible and independent work habit.
- Good communication and team skills, flexibility and stress resistance.
 
Working Conditions :
- Long Term contract
- 39 hrs/Week 5days/week
- Place to work: Glories, Barcelona
Others: 
Good economic conditions
Extensive training on market leading products, personal development programs and mentoring to fully develop skills.
Good career prospects in a reputable company which rewards serious and hard workers.
Full time positions.
