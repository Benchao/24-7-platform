Summary
Title: Evaluator
Description:
Can you name 3 companies where you have recently had a good experience ? Now can you name 3 companies where you have recently received a bad experience ? Most people find it much easier to name the bad ones, yet many companies present themselves as customer centric, but many of them are still failing in delivering great customer experiences. By joining Helion Research as a Mystery Shopper you will be able to evaluate and help the services you may or not use to improve. Have you ever had a bad service experience ? Or have you ever been treated so well you think that a staff member should be rewarded ? Mystery Shopping is the perfect opportunity to get your feedback straight to the top and make a difference. As a Helion Evaluator you will experience a job that is unlike many others ! You will visit shops, restaurants, hotels, show rooms ( the list goes on )that you may not usually visit. Travel to places you may not usually explore on modes of transport that you may never have used before and get paid to do it all ! Mystery shopping is a job that you can fit around your personal agenda. You will only need to accept the work that you can feel you can complete in the time you have free, so ideal for any personal circumstance. On the other hand mystery shopping is not a full time employment. It’s a perfect way to earn some extra money. Whenever you take part in a Helion program you will give yourself a chance to win one of the many fabulous prizes. These prices will be given at selected moments during the year. So to put in into a few simple points, following requirements are needed to become a Helion Evaluator. 18 years or older An eye for detail Able to work with deadlines Good communication skills Good written skills Access to a computer with internet access Contactable by phone And last but not least… You need to enjoy yourself Visit our websites www.helionresearch.com and get started as a Helion Evaluator.
Required languages: (SPANISH-good, ENGLISH-basic)
