Title: Team Leader for Business Travel in Spain Arbeitsort: Barcelona
Description:
Talent Search People is a Consulting Company specialized in the Recruitment & Outsourcing of IT & commercial people & native speakers. Our client is a well-known Multinational Company specialized in Business Travels. It is a company with three divisions: Business Travels, Groups & Events and Congresses Management. We are looking for a German speaking Team Leader for a Business Travel Team (m/f) AREA OF RESPONSIBILITY: The main responsibility is to Lead a team of travel counsellors located in Barcelona and take the Leadership of a German Emergency Travel Service team devoted to solve any urgent issue arising from business travellers taking place anywhere in the World as part of a Multilingual and International Emergencies working group: -Driving a consistently high level of service to our customers through people leadership and delivery of consistent achievement of key performance metrics. -Coordination with Local Key Contacts in each market. Build and maintain a highly skilled team through recruitment and implementation of a continuous skills improvement program. -Using strong performance management, including regular active coaching and training sessions, the team leader will develop an effective best in class team of travel counsellors. -Build strong, open and honest relationships and constantly look for opportunities to motivate and inspire the team to deliver high quality customer service. Take actions as opportunities to exceed Customer expectations. -Continuous analysis and use of all available metrics to drive the team's performance forward. Ensure effective use of resources. Anticipate and deal with the day to day recruiting issues to ensure service. -Continuously develops personal skills in key areas: leadership, technical, travel and customer service. -Recommend improvements to the service (Process, Technology and People) to the Operations Manager / Director. -Extraordinarily, attend to client meetings. THE PROFILE WE ARE LOOKING FOR: -Excellent level of German. -Previous experience as a Team Leader will be highly apreciated. -Flexibilty regarding working hours. Working hours will be in the afternoons including weekends. -Excellent interpersonal and problem solving skills. -Travel Industry knowledge and ideally AMADEUS GDS & technology experience. -Excellent communication ability including written and oral presentation skills. -Strong attention to detail and analytical skills. -Excellent administrative and time management skills. -Strong PC knowledge including related PowerPoint/Excel/Microsoft Project/Email skills. -Ability to work with cross-functional teams and to influence both internally and externally. -Natural leader able to inspire and motivate a team of travel consultants. -Outstanding ability to deliver excellent customer service. -Strong People and Communication Skills. -Commitment to going above and beyond for clients. -Highly organized. -Clear thinker and decision-maker. -Strong analytical and numerical skills. -Excellent knowledge of English highly desirable. WHAT'S ON OFFER: - Lunch tickets - Private healthcare insurance - Permanent contract immediately with 2 months probation period - Immediate incorporation - International working environment -Extensive benefit package YOUR APPLICATION: If interested please send us your CV in English referring to "ZAV1-Spanien: "Team leader for a Business Travel Team" e-mail: cstax@talentsearchpeople.co
